Colour Palette

A colour palette is simply a range of colours. When I designed my website I had to consider what colours I liked and thus come up with a set few of unique colours that I could use to keep my website consistant. However before I could go splashing my colours over the webiste I needed to check the contrast ratio to pass WCAG AAA standards. See below for my color palette.

Summary

I chose these colours because they are the closest representation of what the colours I imagined my website would look like. I found my colours from Color Hunt and edited them to suite the WCAG AAA standards.
